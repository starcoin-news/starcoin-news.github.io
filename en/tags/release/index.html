Starcoin Blockchain release v0.5

Published at September 20, 2020 ·  2 min read

Starcoin Blockchain released v0.5, which is a major refactoring of the Block, Genesis, Stdlib modules, and implements the new Service Registry framework and adds a new Consensus Algorithm.
